# Pizza Sales KPIs, Trends and Insights
I have used the pizza_sales.csv [dataset](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/17U0ah6Q4MJM_wIn_Xl4fHc-1fO6Q4s6z) provided by [Data Tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/@datatutorials1) for this analysis.
## Business Requirements
The dataset consists of sales data from January 2015 to December 2015 of different pizzas of different sizes and categories. The following are the requirements that are asked:
### KPIs
<ul style="list-style-type: circle;">
  <li>Total Revenue</li>
  <li>Average Order Value (Average Revenue per Order)</li>
  <li>Total Pizzas Sold</li>
  <li>Total Orders</li>
  <li>Average Pizzas per Order</li>
</ul>

### Charts
<ul style="list-style-type: circle;">
  <li>Daily and Monthly trends of Total Orders</li>
  <li>Percentage of Sales by Pizza Categories and Sizes</li>
  <li>Total Pizza Sold per Category</li>
  <li>Top 5 Best Seller Pizzas by Revenue, Quantity Sold and Total Orders</li>
  <li>Bottom 5 Seller Pizzas by Revenue, Quantity Sold and Total Orders</li>
</ul>
